About Us

Leonardo is a relaxing, quality country bar run by award-winning owners in the beautiful Berkshire village of Peasemore, just a short drive from the A34 and junctions 13 and 14 of the M4. The building and decor are charming and rustic, with stylish modern touches. Relax by the stove, ideal for winter evenings, or enjoy an outside terrace overlooking the fields or in a pretty flower-filled patio in the summer months.
